How We Started

A Story of Love and Flour

It all started in Ana and Carlos's small apartment in Madrid. It was 2023, we were 25 years old, and we were tired of jobs that didn't fulfill us. One rainy afternoon, while baking cookies for a dinner with friends, we realized something: when people tasted our cookies, they smiled. Really smiled. Not that polite smile, but that smile that only comes when something surprises you pleasantly.

We started selling to friends, then to friends of friends. Soon, our small oven couldn't keep up. The secret ingredient? Love, yes, but also a lot of experimentation. We wanted cookies that not only tasted good but were instagrammable. That people would want to share, show off, and be proud of.

Today, Veo, Veo is not just a cookie shop. It's a team of 6 young, passionate people with a clear mission: to sweeten people's special moments. From that first batch until today, we've learned that the best thing isn't selling cookies, it's being part of our customers' happy moments.
